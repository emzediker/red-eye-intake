Eyecare Teledoc Intake

Important Information:

This optometric telehealth tool is designed to help identify patients who may be appropriate for virtual care for non-emergency eye conditions (such as irritation, allergy, or stye).

Some eye symptoms may indicate more serious conditions that require an in-person evaluation. Pain—especially when associated with light sensitivity or vision changes—should be evaluated by an eye care professional in person.

This questionnaire does not provide a diagnosis. It is used to determine whether telehealth may be appropriate for your situation.
